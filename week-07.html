Week 7: Demand-Side Theories

Who votes for the far-right and why? Grievances and attitudes.

  • Readings
  • Whiteboard

Lecture

  • Download Lecture Slides

Seminar

Objective: To translate your initial “Voter Hypotheses” (from Weeks 2-6) into formal theoretical categories (Economic Grievance, Cultural Backlash, Status Anxiety) and identify regional patterns in Demand.

Seminar Preparation

Look at the “Voter Hypothesis” column from your original regional weeks. What did you write? (e.g., “People were angry about the 2008 crisis” or “Older people didn’t like the new values”).

In Class activities
