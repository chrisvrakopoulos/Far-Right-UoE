Part 1: Why Scandinavia?

The Scandinavian Puzzle

Should be resistant to far-right:

  • Generous welfare states

  • Low inequality

  • High trust societies

  • Strong social democratic tradition

  • Consensus politics

Yet far-right succeeded:

  • Sweden: 20%+ (2018-)

  • Denmark: 21% peak (2015)

  • Norway: 22% peak (2009)

  • Finland: 19% (2011, 2019)
